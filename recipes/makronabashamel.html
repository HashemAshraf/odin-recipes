Makrona Bashamel

Home

This is a delicious Egyptian classic that everyone loves!

Description:

Makrona Bashamel is a traditional Egyptian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and a creamy sauce. It's baked to perfection and served hot.

ingredients:

  1. 1 cup pasta shells
  2. 2 cups cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  3. 1 cup heavy cream
  4. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 1 teaspoon salt
  6. 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook the pasta shells according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat. Add the minced garlic, salt, and black pepper. Stir until well combined.
  4. Add the cheese to the saucepan and stir until it melts and forms a smooth sauce.
  5. In a baking dish, layer the cooked pasta shells and pour the cheese sauce over them. Make sure to cover all the pasta with the sauce.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your delicious Makrona Bashamel!
