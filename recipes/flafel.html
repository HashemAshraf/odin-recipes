Flafel

Home

Description

this is a delicious Egyptian classic that everyone loves!

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup dried chickpeas
  2. 1 small onion, chopped
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  5. 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  6. 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  7. 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  8. 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  9. Salt and pepper to taste
  10. Vegetable oil for frying

Steps:

  • Soak the dried chickpeas in water overnight. Drain and rinse them before use.
  • In a food processor, combine the soaked chickpeas, chopped onion, minced garlic, parsley, cilantro, cumin, coriander, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper. Process until you get a coarse mixture. Be careful not to over-process; you want it to have some texture.
  • Form the mixture into small balls or patties, about the size of a golf ball.
  • Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan or fryer to 350°F (175°C). Fry the falafel balls in batches until they are golden brown and crispy on the outside, about 3-4 minutes per batch. Remove them with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  • Serve the falafel hot with pita bread, tahini sauce, and your favorite toppings such as lettuce, tomatoes, and cucumbers. Enjoy your delicious falafel!
